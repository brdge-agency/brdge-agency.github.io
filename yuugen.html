yuugen yuugen yuugen

yuugen is a DJ and producer mainly focused on Future Funk and J-POP/J-ELECTRO genres. He started in music 2016 producing for artists especially in the hip hop field. In 2020 he started his solo career releasing mixtapes and collaborations with rappers and singers like MEZZ and 杏夏.

In yuugen's tracks you can easily notice the influences of artists like Moe Shop, Night Tempo and YOASOBI. He currently focuses on innovating from digital sound, using both samples and synthesizers and is guided by the slogan "bring the past to the present, to turn it into the future".
